The Fastest Tennis Shots

Serves, Forehands, and Backhands.

Andy Roddick's Serve

FASTEST SERVE

Serves
155 MPH (250 KMH)
Location

Davis Cup - 2004

This serve was hit by Andy Roddick, clocking a speed of 155 MPH. This serve saved Roddick a match point, which in turn helped him win the match.

Gael Monfils' Forehand

FASTEST FOREHAND

Forehands
124 MPH (199 KMH)
Location

Indian Wells - 2022

Nick Kyrgios' record for the quickest forehand was broken by Gael Monfils. In the Indian Wells Masters match versus Carlos Alcaraz, the French player stunned the crowd with a forehand speed of 124 mph. Despite losing to the Spaniard in their Round of 16 match, his enormous forehand left the audience in amazement.

Rafael Nadal's Backhand

FASTEST BACKHAND

Backhands
102 MPH (164 KMH)
Location

US Open - 2009

Former World number 1, Rafael Nadal, hit an extremely fast 102 MPH backhand, spinning at a speed of over 3,000 RPM. This backhand stunned the crowd, and has still not been taken down as the record for the fastest backhand since 2009.
